Our Pledge

The Forge Commitment

Not words on paper — a standard to live by. Before God and my brothers, I make this commitment.

1

I will pursue spiritual connection daily

Seeking God as I understand Him — through prayer, scripture, and honest reflection, wherever I find truth — not out of duty, but desire.

"You will seek me and find me when you seek me with all your heart." — Jeremiah 29:13

2

I will live without masks

Speaking honestly about my struggles, my failures, and my hopes — letting my brothers know the real me.

"Confess your sins to each other and pray for each other so that you may be healed." — James 5:16

3

I will strengthen my mind

Seeking growth, education, and help when I need it — because fortitude means facing what most men avoid.

"Be transformed by the renewing of your mind." — Romans 12:2

4

I will train my body

Treating physical discipline as spiritual discipline — stewarding my health through science and faith alike.

"Do you not know that your bodies are temples of the Holy Spirit?" — 1 Corinthians 6:19

5

I will stand with my brothers

Through loss, divorce, and hardship — showing up when it matters most, carrying weight that is too heavy for one man.

"Carry each other's burdens, and in this way you will fulfill the law of Christ." — Galatians 6:2

6

I will move forward with purpose

Refusing to be defined by what I've lost — pressing on toward who I am called to become.

"Forgetting what is behind and straining toward what is ahead, I press on toward the goal." — Philippians 3:13-14

Ready to Make It?

This commitment is for men done with half-measures. Men over 21, ready to show up.
