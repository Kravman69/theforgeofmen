Our Mission

The Forge of Men exists to create a brotherhood where men over 21 are forged in Christ through Spiritual Connection, Mental Fortitude, and Physical Strength. We provide a non-denominational space for real conversation, personal growth, and human connection — helping men rediscover who they are, what they stand for, and how to move forward with purpose after loss, divorce, or hardship.

"He said, 'Look! I see four men walking around in the fire, unbound and unharmed, and the fourth looks like a son of the gods.'"
— Daniel 3:25

Forged Through Three Pillars

Spiritual Connection

Real conversation and shared faith, with Christ at the core, honoring universal truths across all faiths.

Mental Fortitude

Personal growth and resilience through honesty, accountability, and mental health education that equips men with real tools for success.

Physical Strength

Discipline of the body as a training ground for discipline of the spirit — optimizing health and wellness through science and cutting-edge research.

"Though one may be overpowered, two can defend themselves. A cord of three strands is not quickly broken."
— Ecclesiastes 4:12
