Low-Cost & No-Cost Help

Money should never keep a man from getting help

SAMHSA National Helpline

1-800-662-4357 · Free, 24/7

National referral line for mental health and substance-use treatment — they connect you to low-cost options near you.

BYU Comprehensive Clinic

Provo · (801) 422-7759

Individual, couples, and family counseling for the Utah County community — about $15 per session, reduced further for financial need.

1190 N 900 E, Provo (John Taylor Building)

Mountainlands Community Health Center

Provo · (801) 429-2000

Medical, dental, and mental health care with income-based fees. Helps with Medicaid and insurance enrollment too.

589 S State St, Provo

Wasatch Behavioral Health

Utah County · (801) 373-4760

Utah County's public mental health authority — sliding-scale fees and Medicaid accepted.

NAMI Utah

Free support groups

Free support groups and education for anyone affected by mental health challenges — for you or for family walking it with you.
